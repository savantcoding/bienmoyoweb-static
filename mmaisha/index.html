mMaisha Mobile Health Platform

mMaisha is Bienmoyo's flagship product supporting health systems-strengthening efforts. It is an integrated, web-based suite of tools to manage clinical data, client workflows, and administrative tasks. It is cross-platform and accessible from netbook/laptop computers, tablets, and browser-capable smartphones.

Total clients currently registered in mMaisha:

5300

eForms

Encrypted submission to central server makes data accessible from anywhere once it is entered
Data validation helps prevent errors and increase efficiency
Fully-translatable interface can be easily extended for regional needs
